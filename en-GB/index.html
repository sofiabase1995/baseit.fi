Our Services

BASEiT Oy is a subsidiary of Finnish HR company BASE Henkilöstöpalvelut Oy. BASEiT Oy offers IT consulting services as well as personnel solutions such as temporary staff, freelancers and IT subcontracting and direct recruitment services. Below you can find out about our personnel solution services, which are suitable for both short-term and long-term projects and assignments.

Direct recruiting

Direct recruitment service, best suited for long-term projects and assignments. Leave us a job advertisement and let us find the best professional for you in our international network.

Temporary employment

Temporary labor is often a suitable option when the nature of the project is temporary, or it is otherwise a form of recruitment suitable for the Customer. In this case the specialist is on BASEiT Oy's payroll and works for the Customer.

IT subcontracting

BASEiT Oy offers subcontracting services for IT project management, service design consulting and software development projects in Finland and Scandinavia. You can buy expert work or project management services from us for both short-term and long-term projects.

Further training and courses

BASEiT offers an extensive and constantly renewed training offer for IT professionals in both technical and management terms. Additional training can be found in either express or extensive implementation. Grab this great opportunity right away so you can meet the needs of future challenges better tomorrow.

Why BASEiT Oy?

BASEiT Oy is a modern HR and consulting company with new and stable technologies, long-term international experience, and an extensive network of IT professionals at its core.

In this way, BASEiT Oy can implement personnel solutions for the various business needs of IT companies: from project planning to its implementation.
